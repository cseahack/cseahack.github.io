About The Event

An inter-department hackathon is conducted by CSEA and
sponsored by ACM for students of all departments.

F.A.Q

  • What is a hackathon?

    A hackathon is day-long effort to build or invent something fun and awesome as a team.

  • Who can attend this event?

    It is open to all students excluding first years and final years.

  • Do I need any specific technical skills in order to attend?

    Knowledge of coding is not essential. Ideal teams would be made up of participants with a variety of skills.

  • Are there prizes?

    Yes indeed. Prizes worth Rs 6000 will be awarded to top 3 teams. Every other contestant will be provided with participation certificate.

  • How much does it cost to attend?

    The hackathon is free to attend.

  • What should I bring?

    Yourself, and a laptop.

  • Do i need to register?

    Yes - Click on register from top menu.
